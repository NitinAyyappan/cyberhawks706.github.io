What We Do

We are Arrowhead High School's Robotics Team 706 from Hartland, Wisconsin. We compete under the FIRST organization at the FRC (grades 9-12) level. Every year, FIRST provides us with a new game challenge. We then brainstorm, prototype, design, construct and program to build a robot to compete at regional competitions around the Midwest.

Build

We brainstorm, prototype, and use computer aided design to create the robot itself.

Programming

We write code for the robot using JAVA and complete the robot's wiring.

Business

We create social media content and promotional materials, communicate with sponsors, and track income and expenses.

Outreach

We promote STEM through demonstrations, mentorship, and partnerships with schools and community organizations.

What is FIRST Robotics Competition?

Each year, teams of high school students, coaches, and mentors work during a six-week period to build a robot capable of taking part in an international robotics competition. Teams can win various awards and scholarships, as well as spread STEM ideas across high schools everywhere.
